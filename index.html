About the Museum

The East Asian History Museum explores everyday and sacred histories across China, Korea, Japan, and neighboring regions. We preserve not only objects but also the stories around them—how they were made, used, traded, and remembered. We link material culture with oral histories and community memory, helping visitors see how the past continues to shape daily life today.
